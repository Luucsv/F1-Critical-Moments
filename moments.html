Round 1 — Bahrain Grand Prix
The season opener sets the tone
Verstappen overtakes Hamilton on lap 53 at Turn 4 but is ordered to give the position back after exceeding track limits. Hamilton wins, but the fierce rivalry immediately ignites. Both drivers leave Bahrain separated by just a handful of points.
Hamilton wins — 25 pts

Round 2 — Emilia Romagna Grand Prix
Verstappen blocks, Hamilton spins
At Tosa corner, Verstappen denies Hamilton passage in a fierce wheel-to-wheel battle. Hamilton runs wide off track, spins through the gravel, and damages his front wing. Verstappen takes a dominant win and the championship lead for the first time.
Verstappen wins — 26 pts
🇮🇹
R2
Round 6 — Azerbaijan Grand Prix
Verstappen's tyre blowout costs the lead
Leading with just 5 laps remaining on the Baku street circuit, Verstappen suffers a catastrophic rear tyre blowout at over 300 km/h on the pit straight. He somehow brings the car to a stop safely. Pérez inherits the win for Red Bull, and Hamilton misses a chance to score heavily after braking too late at the restart.
Pérez wins — Verstappen DNF
🇦🇿
R6
Round 10 — British Grand Prix
Hamilton crashes Verstappen on lap 1
At the high-speed Copse corner on the very first lap, Hamilton attempts a move on the inside. The two cars make contact and Verstappen is sent barrel-rolling into the barrier at enormous speed. He is taken to hospital for checks. Hamilton receives a 10-second penalty but still wins the race to the delight of the home crowd.
Hamilton wins — Verstappen hospitalized
🇬🇧
R10
Round 14 — Italian Grand Prix
Both title rivals crash out at Monza
After Hamilton exits the pit lane just ahead of Verstappen following a safety car, both fight aggressively for position into the chicane. Verstappen's car mounts over Hamilton's Mercedes, with the Red Bull's tyre coming to rest on Hamilton's helmet — protected only by the halo device. Both drivers retire. Ricciardo wins for McLaren in an emotional home win for the team.
Ricciardo wins — Both DNF
🇮🇹
R14
Round 19 — São Paulo Grand Prix
Hamilton's greatest comeback drive
Starting 10th after a grid penalty for an engine change, Hamilton produces one of the most breathtaking drives of his career. He battles through the entire field and passes Verstappen around the outside at Turn 4 in an iconic overtake, going on to win by over 10 seconds in what many consider the drive of the decade.
Hamilton wins from P10
🇧🇷
R19
Round 21 — Saudi Arabian Grand Prix
Controversy in Jeddah — level on points
In an extraordinarily chaotic race featuring two red flags, Verstappen deliberately slows in front of Hamilton causing a rear-end collision. Despite the incident and a 5-second penalty for Verstappen, Hamilton wins. Going into the final race, both drivers are tied on points — the first time since 1974 the championship comes down to the wire this way.
Hamilton wins — tied on points

Round 22 — Abu Dhabi Grand Prix
Verstappen wins the title on the final lap
In one of the most controversial finishes in F1 history, race director Michael Masi allows only the lapped cars between Hamilton and Verstappen to unlap themselves under a late safety car. Verstappen, on fresh soft tyres, overtakes Hamilton on the very last lap. He wins his first World Drivers' Championship by just 8 points — the closest possible margin after 22 races.
Verstappen — 2021 World Champion
